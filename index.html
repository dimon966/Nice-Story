Real Italian gelato made with natural ingredients. We have been creating ice cream with love for over 15 years, using only fresh fruit, real cream and proven family recipes.

We care about you!

The history of our family business began in 1998, when grandmother Maria first prepared ice cream according to an Italian recipe. Today we preserve traditions, combining them with modern technologies.

We believe: ice cream should bring joy! That is why we carefully select each ingredient and control quality at all stages of production.

Our ice cream is not just a dessert, it is a real magic of taste. We use only natural milk from Ukrainian farms and fresh seasonal fruits.

Each portion contains the love and care of our masters, who have been perfecting the recipes for three generations.
